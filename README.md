Privacy Policy — Gaume
Last updated: June 16, 2026
1. Who we are
Gaume is a mobile app for writing and sharing personal thoughts. The data controller is Gaume Compagny (gaumecompagny@gmail.com).
2. Data we collect
We only collect the data needed for the app to work:
Account: email address and username, provided at sign-up.
Content: the thoughts you write in the app, and the colors and fonts you choose for them.
Profile photo (optional): if you add a profile picture, we access your photo library or camera solely for that purpose. We never access them otherwise.
Notifications: a push notification token, used to send you reminders and notify you of friend activity.
We do not collect location data, microphone data, or your contacts.
3. How we use your data
Your data is used only to:
Create and manage your account
Display your content and your friends' content in the app
Send push notifications (reminders, friend activity)
We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for advertising. There is no advertising and no algorithmic ranking — you only see what you and the people you choose share.
4. Storage and security
Data is stored on Supabase servers (secure, GDPR-compliant infrastructure). All communication is encrypted via HTTPS.
5. Data retention
Your data is kept as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all your data by contacting us by email.
6. Your rights
Under the GDPR, you have the right to:
Access your data
Rectify or delete it
Object to processing
Data portability
To exercise these rights, contact us at: gaumecompagny@gmail.com
7. Contact
For any question about this policy: gaumecompagny@gmail.com
